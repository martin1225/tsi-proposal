Why This Strategy?

Guerrilla Visibility

Kota Bharu is a driving city. Every car sticker is a moving billboard. By targeting our top customers, we ensure our brand is seen in affluent areas.

Status & Exclusivity

Scarcity drives desire. By limiting stickers to "Top 5" lists, we gamify the shopping experience. Customers will compete to stay on top.

Ecosystem Lock-in

Cross-pollination. A construction client (TSI) becomes a food customer (Layang) because the VIP perks follow them across the group.

The "Top 5" Mechanism

We don't give stickers to everyone. To maintain prestige, eligibility is strictly performance-based.

1

The Legends (Cumulative)

Top 5 cumulative spenders since Jan 1, 2023. This honors long-term loyalty and stability.

2

The Challengers (Monthly)

Top 5 spenders of the current month. This encourages new customers to "chase" the leaderboard.

!

The "No-Hogging" Rule

1 Customer = 1 Sticker. If a winner already has a sticker, the reward passes to the next eligible person. Maximum fleet distribution.
